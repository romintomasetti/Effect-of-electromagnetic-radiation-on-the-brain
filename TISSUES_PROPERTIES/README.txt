The files contained in this folder are properties (thermal and electromagnetic)
of human body tissues. Sources are:

1) Files with termination '_ITIS': https://www.itis.ethz.ch/virtual-population/tissue-properties/database/database-summary/ [ACCESSED ON 24 APRIL 2018]

2) Files with termination '_IFAC': http://niremf.ifac.cnr.it/tissprop/htmlclie/htmlclie.php [ACCESSED ON 24 APRIL 2018]
